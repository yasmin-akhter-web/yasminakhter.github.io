About

I am a tenure-track Assistant Professor of 19th and 20th Century Anglophone Literature in the Department of English at the University of Iowa. I earned my PhD from the Department of English at the University of Virginia, where I was a Fellow at the Jefferson Scholars Foundation from 2020 to 2025. I also serve as the Founder of the Global Decadence Lab in the Decadence Research Centre at Goldsmiths, University of London, and as a Co-Director for Undisciplining the Victorian Classroom, a digital publication focused on producing peer-reviewed curricular materials.

I was born and raised in the former British colony of Hong Kong, and also spent my formative years in Vancouver, Canada. Beyond my academic work, I enjoy playing my guitar, swimming, and yoga. I also enjoy circulating amongst the many artistic and literary communities in Iowa City, particularly Prairie Lights and the Wright House of Fashion.
