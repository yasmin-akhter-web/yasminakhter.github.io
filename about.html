About

I am an Early Career Fellow at the Institute of English Studies and recipient of the BARS/BAVS-funded Nineteenth Century Matters Fellowship (University of Edinburgh, 2025-26). I received my PhD from the Department of English at Royal Holloway, University of London, where I was Co-Director of the Centre for Victorian Studies.
